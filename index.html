About Mahek

Architecture · Art · Writing

I am an architecture student at Illinois Tech in Chicago, pursuing a B.Arch with a specialization in digital design and a minor in English Language and Linguistics. Having studied architecture across India, the United States, and Japan, I am interested in how place, culture, structure, and language shape the way we understand design.

My approach to architecture is rooted in long contemplation — the belief that a building must be considered before it is drawn, so it can carry meaning, sit lightly on its environment, and make people feel something when they walk in. I am drawn to long-term impact: to sustainable design, the careful use of AI as a tool, and the ambition of work that earns a place in the stories of the cities it sits in.
